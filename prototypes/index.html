Visualizations

Each group must produce at least one unique interactive multi-component visualization page per person using D3.js version 5 (using older versions will not receive full credit). Visualizations should be both informative and aesthetically pleasing. The individual components on the visualization page should have their interactivity linked. For example, highlighting a category in one component should highlight that same category in all components.

Keep the required screen space to view your visualizations within 1440 x 900 pixels. These are maximums; your visualizations do not need to take up that entire space and do not need to have that aspect ratio. Your visualizations do not need to be responsive to the browser size.
